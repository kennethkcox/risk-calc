Guide to Quantitative Risk Analysis

Financials & Frequency

Model uncertainty using a PERT distribution (Min, Most Likely, Max) for both financial loss and event frequency to produce a realistic, weighted average.

Key Metrics (ALE)

ALE (Annualized Loss Expectancy) is the total expected financial loss per year. It's calculated from SLE (loss from one event) and ARO (events per year).

Control Effectiveness

The combined effectiveness of your implemented ISO 27001 controls creates a Residual Risk Modifier. This single modifier is applied to both the financial loss (SLE) and event frequency (ARO) to calculate the final "residual" risk.
