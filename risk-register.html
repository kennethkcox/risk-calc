Guide to Risk Analysis

Likelihood & Impact

Assess risks by selecting a Likelihood (how often it might happen) and an Impact (how bad it would be). These values are used to calculate a simple risk score.

Risk Score

The Risk Score is calculated by multiplying the Likelihood and Impact values. This score determines the overall risk level (Low, Medium, High, or Critical).

Control Effectiveness

Applying ISO 27001 controls reduces the calculated Likelihood of a risk. The average effectiveness of your applied controls creates a modifier that lowers the final risk score.
